Overview

Welcome to ASCEND — the AI-powered fitness performance operating system for serious athletes. This Privacy Policy explains how ASCEND Performance OS ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our website at ascendapp.in and our Android application.

By accessing or using ASCEND, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

Data We Collect

We collect data to provide you with a personalised and optimised training experience. The information we may collect includes:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Fitness & Training Data: Workout logs, sets, reps, weight, RPE, personal records, kinematic load metrics, and CNS recovery scores you enter into the app.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and crash reports.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, session duration, and interaction patterns within the app and website.
  • Health Data: If you connect third-party services such as Apple Health, STRAVA, WHOOP, Garmin, or Fitbit, we may receive biometric and activity data from those platforms subject to your permissions.
  • Communications: If you contact us via email at contact@ascendapp.in, we retain those communications to respond to your enquiries.

We do not collect payment information directly. Any future transactions will be processed through secure third-party payment providers.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to improve your training experience and operate our service. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the ASCEND app and website.
  • Personalise your training dashboard, PR tracking, and recovery recommendations.
  • Analyse aggregate usage patterns to improve features and performance.
  • Send transactional emails such as account confirmation or support responses.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

ASCEND integrates with several third-party platforms to enhance your performance tracking. When you connect these services, their own privacy policies govern how they handle your data:

  • Apple Health — Apple Privacy Policy
  • STRAVA — Strava Privacy Policy
  • WHOOP — WHOOP Privacy Policy
  • Garmin — Garmin Privacy Statement
  • Fitbit / Google — Google Privacy Policy

We only request access to data you explicitly authorise when connecting these services. You may revoke access at any time through your device or third-party account settings.

Analytics & Tracking

We use Google Analytics (GA4) on our website to understand how visitors interact with our content. Google Analytics collects anonymised data such as pages visited, time on page, browser type, and general geographic location. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We do not use advertising trackers, retargeting pixels, or any third-party ad networks. ASCEND is an ad-free experience.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and regular security reviews to protect your information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss.

While we take data security seriously, no system is 100% immune to risk. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your ASCEND account and to keep it confidential.

Data is retained for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.
  • Objection: Object to how we process your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@ascendapp.in. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

ASCEND is designed for serious athletes and is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it promptly. If you believe we may have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our services evolve. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of ASCEND after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please reach out to us:

  • Email: contact@ascendapp.in
  • Website: www.ascendapp.in
  • Location: India

We are committed to resolving any privacy concerns promptly and transparently.
